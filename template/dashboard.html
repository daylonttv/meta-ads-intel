Gardner's vs. Industry

TW + Polar + Varos
"F&B Bench" = typical range for Food & Beverage DTC brands. "All Ecom" = cross-industry median. Sources: Polar Analytics (4K+ Shopify brands), Varos (4,500+ brands), MHI Media (1,247 Meta accounts), Ryze AI, and Immerss.
Blended ROAS 3.43× is solid — above the all-ecom median (2.87×) and within the F&B range (3–5×). Our overall marketing mix is working.

Meta ROAS 2.10× is below where we should be. The F&B floor is 2.5×. On Normal days we averaged 2.21× — still below, but platform instability accounts for ~12% of the gap.

Meta CPA $35.88 is at the top of the F&B range ($15–35). On Normal days: $34.82 (within range). On Bad/Very Bad days: $40.15 (above even the all-ecom median).

Meta CPM $9.86 is 21% above F&B bench ($8.14) but well below US average ($23.00).

Bottom line: Blended is healthy. Meta is underperforming vs. F&B benchmarks — roughly half explained by platform instability, the rest is a real optimization opportunity.

Macro Events × Our Performance

News + TW
When something big happens in the world, it affects our ads in two ways. First, people's Facebook and Instagram feeds fill up with news, outrage, and commentary — which means they're doomscrolling instead of shopping. Second, economic events (gas prices, inflation reports, tariff news) directly affect whether people feel comfortable spending on non-essentials like specialty cheese. This section tracks both: what was dominating feeds and what was hitting wallets.
