Macro Events × Our Performance

News + TW
When something big happens in the world, it affects our ads in two ways. First, people's Facebook and Instagram feeds fill up with news, outrage, and commentary — doomscrolling displaces product discovery. Ad impressions still serve but conversion intent drops. Second, economic events (gas prices, inflation, tariff news) affect whether people feel comfortable spending on non-essentials. A ↓ suppresses spending tag means the event was a mood/wallet negative. A ↑ boosts spending tag means gift intent was elevated. The 📱 HIGH badge means feeds were saturated for 2+ days. Green vertical lines on charts = gift-giving holidays.
